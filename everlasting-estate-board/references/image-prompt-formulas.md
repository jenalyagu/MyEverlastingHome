## Full board prompt

Top section: [HERO EXTERIOR]. Middle section: refined floor-plan fantasy panels labeled MAIN FLOOR, UPPER FLOOR, WELLNESS LEVEL, and ESTATE SITE PLAN. Include clean, soft architectural plan graphics with numbered zones, pale stone paper texture, fine black linework, muted landscaping, and minimal labels. Lower section: two horizontal strips of photorealistic lifestyle vignettes showing [INTERIOR SPACES] and [LIFESTYLE MOMENTS]. Bottom section: SCIP/resilience advantage icons with short refined captions. Use elegant serif typography, small caps labels, warm ivory background, limestone beige, muted sage, charcoal ink, soft gold accents, high-end editorial layout, refined spacing, premium family legacy mood. Keep all text minimal and legible.
